This policy is framed in order to aid the student and researcher community in this harder times and also in the future, to be able to assess the pandemic situation using all the data available in the public and private domain for the benifit of humanity.Therefore, this policy is aimed to improve the global resilience and encourage people to share knowledge and promote the true spirit of humanity.
